01 / Before you arrivePrerequisites

Come ready to commit config, not to learn the CLI.

The curriculum moves fast. Both items below are expected; finishing the on-demand course meaningfully changes how much you take away from the hands-on labs.

Required

Comfortable with basic SRX CLI & operation

You should be able to navigate Junos operational and configuration modes, edit hierarchies, and commit changes without a cheat-sheet. JNCIA-SEC level or equivalent hands-on time is the baseline.

JNCIA-SEC · or equivalent
Strongly recommended

Complete Juniper Security on-demand before Day 1

Work through the Juniper Learning on-demand track. It covers the zones, policy, NAT and VPN foundations we will build on from the first morning.

02 / Curriculum3-Day Agenda

Foundations Monday. Deployment Tuesday. Advanced & HA Wednesday.

Each day pairs focused lectures with hands-on labs in a personal vLabs pod. Timings are in Singapore time (SGT). Break and lunch slots are held strictly so labs stay on track.

Theme

Get comfortable with Junos on SRX, understand flow-based processing, and build your first working security policy.

Theme

Move from basic policy enforcement to real-world deployment scenarios — NAT, routing integration, and site-to-site / remote-access VPN.

Theme

Layer advanced security services on top of your policies, then make the whole thing highly available and multi-tenant.
